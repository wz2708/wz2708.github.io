2D‑RoPE for Performer: Efficient High‑Resolution Vision Transformers

Graduate Research, Columbia · 2024

Unified 2D rotary embeddings (axial and mixed) within Performer’s FAVOR+ to retain O(N) efficiency while improving spatial fidelity. Observed +1.8 pp Top‑1 on ImageNet‑1K with ~25% lower memory vs. baseline Performer.
